What makes this different

Most trainers cannot see what is holding you back. Marina can.

A standard trainer programs around a body they cannot fully assess. They see the lift. They cannot see the fascial chain that is shortening your range, capping your strength, or shifting load into the joint you are protecting without knowing it.

Marina trained in bodywork for ten years before she started taking PT clients. She sees restriction patterns no standard trainer sees. And she can address them inside the session, with hands-on release before the lift, mobility before the load, and breathwork before the next set.

The result is training that compounds. You are not lifting through restriction. You are building strength in the range your body actually has.
